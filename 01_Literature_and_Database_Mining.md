# SOP 1: Scientific Literature & Database Mining

### 🧬 Background
Before structural modeling can begin, a researcher must isolate the correct biological sequences and review the existing literature surrounding the target. 

### 🛠️ Workflow Steps

**1. Bibliographic Retrieval (NCBI PubMed)**
* Utilize **NCBI PubMed** and **ScienceDirect** to search for target-specific literature.
* *Example Search:* Utilizing Boolean operators to find specific cancer or viral genomics papers.
* Import citations into **Mendeley** or **ReadCube** to maintain an organized, formatted bibliography for research publications.

**2. Sequence Retrieval (Entrez & UniProt)**
* Access the **NCBI Entrez** cross-database search system.
* Extract the primary amino acid sequence (FASTA format) for the target protein. 
* Cross-reference the sequence against **UniProtKB** to verify functional domains, transmembrane regions, and known variants before proceeding to 3D modeling.
